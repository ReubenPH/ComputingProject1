Come Visit Haddington!

The John Gray Centre is a Library and Museum named after John Gray, who established one of the first public libraries in Haddington in 1717. The museum is focused on local history.

Come shop at the Haddington Farmers Market held on the last Saturday of every month. Haddington Pipeband are also often there!

St Mary's Parish Church is the oldest parish church in Scotland, dating back to the 14th century. Walk down the connected river walk along the Tyne.

Relax in St Mary's Pleasance Garden, a beautiful private garden next to Haddington House, dating back to 1648.
